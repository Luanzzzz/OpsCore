# Research Summary: OpsCore

**Date:** 2026-04-17

## Stack

Use a relational, async-friendly architecture with explicit AI and integration boundaries. The core system should optimize for normalized operational data and traceability before optimizing for channel breadth.

## Table Stakes

- Unified inbox for operational entries
- AI-assisted triage with category, urgency and next action
- Task conversion with ownership and status tracking
- Agenda/follow-up coordination
- Dashboard visibility for bottlenecks and pending work

## Differentiators

- Strong linkage across inbox, task and agenda
- Recommendations that help operators decide what to do next
- A wider operational orchestration lens than a simple triage product

## Watch Out For

- Integration-led scope creep
- Vague IA requirements
- Module fragmentation between inbox, tasks and agenda
- Regressing into a smaller InboxFlow-shaped product
